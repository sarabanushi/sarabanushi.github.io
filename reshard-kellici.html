Reshard Kellici

Reshard Kellici is Partner with Frost & Fire Consulting, primarily involved in the corporate department and specialized in the field of energy and natural resources law, providing strategic solutions, structuring and negotiating complex transactions for joint ventures, mergers and acquisitions, traditional and non-recourse financing, venture capital and project development deals across all industries, locally and internationally. Throughout his years of experience with the international law firm Hogan Lovells, and Frost & Fire Consulting, Mr. Kellici has been part of the globally leading and most prestigious practices and clients in their respective sectors. Routinely, Mr. Kellici has had the opportunity to represent some of the largest developers in the world, helping them finance and develop diverse portfolios of energy and infrastructure projects, with a special focus on renewable energy. Additionally, Mr. Kellici has assisted a variety of clients in the development and financing of natural gas pipelines and natural gas-fired generation projects. While negotiating complex agreements and helping clients bring innovative technologies to the market, Mr. Kellici has managed billions of dollars of mergers and acquisitions transactions, across a variety of sectors in a number of countries.
Representative clients throughout the entirety of Mr. Kellici’s practice up to date include General Electric, SunEdison, SolarCity, Tradewind Energy, Enel, Dominion Energy, Covanta Energy, Constellation Energy Group, Ecosystem Investment Partners, ContourGlobal, Venture Global LNG, EDP Renewables, UGI Corporation, Fulcrum Energy, Credit Suisse, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Gener 2, Energy Development Group, Albgaz and a number of governmental agencies and institutions, both in Albania and in the US.

Mr. Kellici was born in Tirana, Albania and has completed his secondary, post-secondary and professional education in the United States. Mr. Kellici received his Juris Doctor (Magna Cum Laude, Order of the Coif) from the University of Maryland School of Law in 2013. Prior to that, Mr. Kellici received his Master of Science degree in Criminal Justice (Magna Cum Laude) in 2007 and his Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Mathematics (Summa Cum Laude) in 2003.
During law school, Mr. Kellici served as an editor for both the Maryland Law Review and the Maryland Business Law Development journal. He also served as a teaching fellow for the Maryland Law Academic Achievement Program and participated in numerous Baltimore City mentoring programs intended to provide underprivileged students with access and support in post-secondary education. Mr. Kellici invests a great deal of his passion in mentoring startups, serving in boards of non-profit organizations and in helping Albanian students prepare for professional life after graduation. He currently serves as a member of the Board of Directors of the Harry T. Fultz Institute and as the General Secretary and Board Member of the American Chamber of Commerce in Albania.
Mr. Kellici is admitted to practice in Maryland and is a member of the Maryland State Bar Association and the American Bar Association. Mr. Kellici may be reached by phone at +355 44500230 or by email at Reshard.Kellici@ffconsulting.al.
