Labor Law

A company should comply with labor laws and regulations not only because people are the most important asset to any company or organization and observing their legal rights is paramount to healthy workforce morale but also because non-compliance can result in penalties, liabilities and public relations issues. The members of our firm have extensive experience as both legal advisors and practitioners of employment issues.

Our employment law services include the following areas:

  • Contracts, Policies and Procedures.Preparation and/or review of offer letters, employment agreements, independent contractor and/or consulting agreements, personnel policies and procedures, handbooks, protection of confidential information and trade secret agreements and compensation agreements and any other workplace related memorandums or documents. Provision of training on these policies in order to ensure managers and employees understand and implement policies and procedures correctly.

  • Legal Compliance.External review of workplace policies, procedures and practices to determine compliance with Albanian Labor Code, Social Security, Health and Safety and other applicable laws. Internal workplace investigations relating to whistleblower and workplace misconduct allegations.

  • Employment-related litigation. Assist with unionizing activities, dealings with works councils at all levels, collective bargaining, interpretation of work rules, industrial disputes, strikes and lockouts.

  • Expatriate Services and Executive Transfer.Provision of end-to-end advice, from obtaining work and residency permits to sorting out employment agreements, social security requirements, tax equalization and other issues.

  • Employee Benefits and Compensation.Design and implementation of all types of employee benefits and compensation programs, including pay-grades, incentive or variable remuneration plans, profit sharing plans and equitybased compensation programs, such as stock options, stock purchasing and others.

  • Workforce Restructuring.Advice at all stages of reductions in workforce from planning to the implementation of notices and separation agreements. (For non-legal restructuring services, please visit our Organization Development service line. (me hyperlink!))

  • Transactions.Advice on employment-related aspects of mergers and acquisitions, spin-offs and divestitures, post-acquisition integrations of newly acquired businesses, as well as outsourcing or other specific arrangements. Employment related due diligence for all types of corporate transactions.
