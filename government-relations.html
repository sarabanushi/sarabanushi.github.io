Government Relations

When determining how to successfully engage with the central and the local government, the critical elements one must consider are (1) having a solid strategic plan, (2) having superior knowledge of the proper legal framework, and (3) gaining exposure to the appropriate decision makers. If your organization can establish these essential underpinnings, your chances for success are greatly enhanced. Based on our deep understanding of political and policy-making processes and drawing on experience gained at various executive levels, our company can assist clients in the ethical representation of their legitimate interests at all levels of the government. We can offer you a roadmap that will allow you to navigate the often bewildering maze of the government. Part of this service is the timely and thorough analysis of public policy issues and related political considerations that enables us to empower our clients to respond to emerging challenges and opportunities. Our professionals provide reliable and informed political insight; build collaborative alliances with other groups; represent clients' interests in high-level meetings with relevant decision makers, administration officials, and their staff; draft proposed legislative and appropriations language; prepare talking points, legislative summaries, advocacy materials, and testimony; and brief decision makers on public policy matters of importance to our clients.

Frost & Fire Consulting brings uncommon experiences, smarts, and execution to the table. If your organization needs help in dealing with a government-related hurdle, we can provide you with the resources, know-how, and energy necessary to achieve success.
