Ardita Hoxha

Ardita Hoxha is Frost & Fire Consulting's office manager, having the responsibility to ensure that everything runs smoothly, including administrative and financial issues.

Prior to joining Frost & Fire Consulting Ms. Hoxha worked in the public and private sector, as well as with international organizations. Her last experience was as a Counterparty Coordinator in a twinning project funded by the European Union (EU) between the Albanian and the Italian Civil Aviation Authority. At the same time she was also an inspector of the Albanian Civil Aviation Authority in the Department of Air Safety. Her responsibilities varied from identifying and analyzing the national legal framework in the area of air safety to monitoring the implementation of the project, ensuring the well-going of the activities in each stage and the achievement of the overall objectives, inspecting international and domestic air carriers to monitor the implementation of safety management systems, and overall ensuring compliance with the acquis communautaire and EU safety standards.

In addition, Ms. Hoxha has considerable experience working in the private sector, including multinational companies such as Heidelberger Druckmaschinen AG. In the framework of the continuous qualification policy of this company, Ardita took part in various seminars & trainings in "Financial & Accounting Programmes", resulting in further enhancing her skills in preparing budgets, analyzing funding means and financial sources, preparing periodic financial reports, monthly payroll, and other declaration of taxes, and ensuring that record keeping meet the requirements of auditors & government agencies pursuant to the applicable law.

Ms. Hoxha was born in Tirana. She graduated at the Faculty of Natural Sciences of the University of Tirana with a Master's Degree in Chemical Engineering. Ardita is fluent in English and Italian and may be reached by phone at +35544500230 or by e-mail at ardita.hoxha@ffconsulting.al.
