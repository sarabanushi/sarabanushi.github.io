Franci Nuri

Franci Nuri is a Senior Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting. Mr. Nuri has spent a considerable amount of his adult life in France and has invested considerably in his legal education while there, in the academia and beyond.

or to joining Frost & Fire Consulting in February 2014, Mr. Nuri worked for about one year as a Legal Counsel at Erytech Pharma SA, a French company listed in the stock exchange, specialized in the area of developing innovative therapies based on red blood cells for treating severe forms of cancer and orphan drugs. During this precious professional experience, Mr. Nuri drafted various agreements with international companies, public or scientific institutions and handled various Trade Marks protections via the WIPO procedures. Earlier, during his studies focused on European Law, Mr. Nuri has also worked as an intern at “Maison de l’Europe de Lyon”, a non-profit organization based in Lyon, France, where he contributed to the development of several activities designed to promote European Institutions. In addition, he has been involved with “Service des Traducteurs d’Urgence”, a French NGO created to provide legal consultations and legal interpretation. During his tenure with Frost & Fire Consulting Mr. Nuri has gained a strong expertise in drafting legal opinions and contracts for private or public entities, in a very wide range of areas which include almost all aspects of the Albanian legal framework. Mr. Nuri’s areas of expertise include, but are not limited to, public procurement legislation, concessions & PPPs, the construction and real estate sector, energy, oil and gas, public and administrative law, industrial and intellectual property rights, tourism and environmental law, European Union law, as well as court cases of civil and administrative nature. He has also been involved in several important investment projects where Albanian legislation on privatization, strategic investments and tourism have been the key components. Recently, Mr. Nuri has been part of various projects with national or international institutions responsible for environmental protection, such as the United Nations Industrial Development Organization, in order to approximate and improve Albanian legislation in line with the European Union Acquis. Furthermore, Mr. Nuri has been licensed by the Albanian Directorate of Industrial Property as a trademark authorized person, a status that enables him to proceed with the registration of domestic and foreign trademarks.

Born in Lushnje, Albania, Mr. Nuri moved at the age of 15 in France where he lived, studied and worked for more than a decade. He completed three years of high school in Lyon, at “Lycée Colbert”, a French public educational institution where Mr. Nuri chose to be formed with priority in the fields of economics and social sciences. He completed his Bachelor of Law Degree with high results at Lyon III University, in Lyon. Subsequently, Mr. Nuri was awarded a Master of 1st Degree in French Public Law in 2010, a Master of 1st Degree in European Law in 2011, and a Master of 2nd Degree in European Business Law (graduating Cum Laude) in 2012, all at Lyon III University, Lyon, France.

Beyond the law, Mr. Nuri likes history, literature, and is interested in socio-economic and political issues. Apart from his native Albanian and French, Mr. Nuri is fluent in English and Italian. Mr. Nuri is admitted to practice in Albania and is a member of the Albanian Bar Association. Mr. Nuri may be reached by phone at +355 4 4500 230, or by email at franci.nuri@ffconsulting.al
