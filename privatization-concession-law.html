Privatization & Concession Law

Despite the notable increase in quality and professionalism among the ranks of the Albanian judiciary in recent years, judicial proceedings in the Albanian court system could be lengthy, tenuous, cumbersome and unpredictable. While judicial reform remains high as a priority in the domestic legislative agenda, we think that it will take some time for the Albanian court system to become truly sophisticated, professional and independent from any outside influence. Considering the current pitfalls of the domestic judicial system, we advise our clients to consider litigation as a means of last resort, and give priority to Alternative Dispute Resolution. However, if and when litigation becomes necessary, Frost & Fire aggressively and qualitatively protects and defends our clients' interest at all levels of the dispute resolution hierarchy.
