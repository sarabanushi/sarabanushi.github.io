About Us

In our Home page we have already stated why Frost & Fire Consulting was created, what we can do, and what our goals, mission, vision and values are.  We take the opportunity in this page to share a bit more about who we are.

The members of our team represent a wide range of backgrounds and interests.  We all have solid academic foundations formed at some of the best programs for the respective disciplines from institutions such as Harvard Law School, New York University School of Law, Columbia Law School, Boston University, Lewis & Clark College, University of Southern Maine, University of Westminster, University of Tirana, University of Maryland-School of Law, etc., and we have all graduated with distinctions.  Furthermore, not only have we had successful careers in our professional capacities, but we have all experienced life outside of a "suit" by working  "real life" jobs ranging from being waiters to working in a foundry.  Most importantly, none of us is all work and no play - among us are former professional basketball players, martial arts champions, divers, violin players and anything else in between.   Yet, despite all of these diverse experiences, we all share important qualities - integrity, creativity, inquisitiveness, energy, dedication, intelligence, and a commitment to excellence.

Working with such a diverse group of people toward our common goal has enabled each and every one of us to reach even higher levels of individual accomplishment while building an even stronger foundation for achieving collective success.
