Agriculture

Although 20 percent of Albania is fertile arable land and agriculture is Albania's largest sector at 25 percent of the GDP, it is yet to modernize. Nevertheless, the potential is considerable, and many investment opportunities have been identified by early investors in medicinal plants and herbs, early- and late-season vegetables, and preserved products such as olives, olive oil, canned tomatoes, fresh and processed fish, and cheese. The Albanian government has set out to increase the financial support for farms and agricultural businesses and to improve the marketing strategy of agricultural products so that Albanian agriculture can achieve a larger market share in the international trade and meet the EU standard requirements for this industry. Frost & Fire Consulting stands ready to assist any potential investors or market operators and to help them achieve their goals drawing from our extensive knowledge of the legislative framework and of the market opportunities and from previous experiences in assisting agriculture giants such as Cargill, Incorporated.
