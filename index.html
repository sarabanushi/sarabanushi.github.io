Frost & Fire Consulting was created with one goal in mind: to offer unparalleled legal, organization development, human resources as well as other business services to clients who operate or wish to explore opportunities in the Albanian or regional market. Our commitment to this goal is rooted in our members' extended and distinguished experience in the private, public and international organization sectors.

We pride ourselves not only in having deep insight into the existing issues and intricacies of the local business climate, but also in being intimately familiar with the expectations and mind-sets of our clients, whether they are foreign investors, multinational corporations, or governmental and international donor agencies. We have succeeded in merging the unique experiences of our members to serve Frost & Fire Consulting's number one priority - superior quality.

Whether you need to use our legal services, human resource management capabilities, or other business or financial-related expertise, we aim to be your consulting firm of choice in Albania and the region.

MISSION

Frost & Fire Consulting is dedicated to deliver uncompromised legal and other business consulting services. Our ultimate mission is to provide our clients with personalized attention, informed advice and practical solutions in accordance with the industry's best practices and the highest standard of fiduciary responsibility. We strive for excellence and aim to exceed expectations by delivering our expertise in such a way that builds long-lasting relationships based on trust, mutual respect, professionalism and partnership.

VISION

To become the most trusted and respected professional services firm in Albania and the wider region, recognized by our clients for delivering excellence, by our competitors as the standard setter and by aspiring young professionals as the best place to develop their talents.

VALUES

If we were to draw a pyramid of Frost & Fire Consulting core values, our commitment to our clients' success would be the foundation, integrity its pillar and quality the all-seeing eye. We value accountability in all of our individual or collective actions. We respect ourselves, our work, and our clients, and we strive to make an impact on society not only through our professional commitments but also through our civic and not-for-profit engagements.
