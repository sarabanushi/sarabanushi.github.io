Enxhi Kallogjeri

Enxhi Kallogjeri is an Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting. Originally from Tirana, she has spent most of her life between Rome, London and Copenhagen while significantly contributing to her educational and vocational path. Ms. Kallogjeri successfully completed her legal studies at the University of Westminster in London where she found herself to be most interest in the area of corporate law. She later continued her vocational training in this field at the University of Law in London.

During her studies, Ms. Kallogjeri complimented her theoretical legal skills by undertaking various summer internships: at a Notary Office in Tirana where she was responsible in assessing the authenticity of documents and maintaining proper records, as a paralegal at a local commercial law firm, and ultimately as an intern at an international law firm where she assisted the managing partners with their workload.
Ms. Kallogjeri's practice areas of interest are banking and finance law, bankruptcy law, company law, private international law, property law and all matters related to business development and foreign investments. She enjoys drafting and scrutinising complex agreements and has worked with all aspects of a legal case, from the initial court motion submissions to initiate court proceedings, to the out of court settlements.

Ms. Kallogjeri likes the fine arts and cultural heritage tourism. She is fluent in English and Italian and has moderate knowledge of Spanish and French. She can be reached by phone at +35544500230 or by e-mail at enxhi.kallogjeri@ffconsulting.al.
