Alfred Rushaj

Alfred Rushaj is a founding member and co-managing partner of Frost & Fire Consulting.   A lawyer by training, throughout his career Mr. Rushaj has been involved in all aspects of the legal environment in Albania, both in the private and public sectors.

Prior to cofounding Frost & Fire Consulting, Mr. Rushaj was Deputy Minister of Finance from 2009 to 2013 and legal advisor to the Minister of Economy, Trade and Energy from 2005 to 2009. From 1997 to 2005 he held several legal, managerial and executive positions with the Albanian Power Corporation (KESH sh.a.). Mr. Rushaj's responsibilities during his career have included being involved with and overseeing the following:

  • The negotiation and drafting of agreements focused on economic cooperation with other countries
  • Drafting and the implementation of the fiscal policies related legislation
  • Soliciting the input of the business community regarding the fiscal policies as member of the Consultative Council for Businesses
  • Coordinating of fiscal policies with international bodies such as the World Bank, the International Monetary Fund, USAID, the European Union, etc.
  • Overseeing the national agencies responsible for revenue collection as well as those responsible for money laundering prevention
  • Approximation of the Albanian fiscal legislation with that of the European Union
  • Negotiating the Trans-Adriatic Pipeline (TAP) on behalf of the Albanian government
  • Drafting legislation related to the privatization of sectors of special importance as decreed by the parliament, including energy, telecommunication and infrastructure and negotiating the privatization of several important public companies
  • Drafting legislation and related administrative acts and documentation concerning concessions at all levels and overseeing the issuance of several concessionary licenses for some of the most important resources in the mining and energy sector
  • Drafting legislation-related administrative acts and documentation concerning the energy sector at all levels, including contracts for distribution and collection, procurement and construction of power stations, interconnection and transmission lines, power supply agreements at wholesale and retail level, as well as legislation that contributed to the creation of the energy market, licensing of third party operators, and increased foreign investment in the sector
  • Restructuring the Albanian Power Company that ultimately resulted in the creation of three different companies responsible for the production, transmission and distribution of the energy

In addition to the above responsibilities, Mr. Rushaj has been Chairman of the Supervisory Board of OSSH sh.a. (the Albanian Power Distribution and Supply Company), member of the Board of Directors of OST sh.a. (the Albanian Transmission System Operator), member of the Supervisory Board of Albtelecom (the Albanian National Telecommunications Company), member of the Supervisory Board of Albpetrol (the Albanian Petroleum Company), Chairman of Supervisory Board of INSIG sh.a. (Albanian Insurance Company), Chairman of Supervisory Board of INSIG Macedonia (Albanian Insurance Company operating in Macedonia), and member of the Board of Directors of the National Natural Resources Agency. He has also taught Public Law as adjunct faculty from 1999 to 2004 at the University of Tirana.

Mr. Rushaj was born in Lezhe, Albania.  He received a law degree from the University of Tirana in 1997, graduating at the top of his class with a 4.0 GPA.  Mr. Rushaj is admitted to practice in Albania and is a member of the Albanian Bar Association.
