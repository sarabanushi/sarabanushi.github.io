Kris Hasa

Kris Hasa is a Junior Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting. He has completed his studies with excellent results and graduated with the highest honors from the Faculty of Law at the University of Tirana, where he also received a Master of Science degree in Civil Law. During his years of study, Mr. Hasa was focused in the area of corporate governance and commercial transactions. Mr. Hasa currently holds the title of Assistant Attorney and pursues the continuing legal education program at the National School of Advocacy.

Prior to joining Frost & Fire Consulting, Mr. Hasa had over two years of experience as a lawyer in a company involved with immovable property registration, analyzing and resolving issues on countless practical cases based on property law, such as the privatization of agricultural land and enterprises, restitution and compensation of property, legalization of unauthorized buildings, etc. Prior to this experience, he completed a one-year internship with a law firm, contributing to drafting lawsuits, contracts, legal opinions, assisting in litigation, and maintaining correspondence with public bodies on behalf of the firm.

Mr. Hasa’s areas of interest include commercial law, private international law, intellectual property and arbitration. Mr. Hasa is certified by GIZ in the Law on Foreign Investments and Related Dispute Resolution Procedures. He has been trained on renewable energy legislation and approximation of legislation in the field of European Company Law.

Mr. Hasa is a researcher and contributor to the doctrine of Albanian private law, being the author of several articles published in law journals.

Beyond the law, Mr. Hasa likes literature and philosophy. He was born in Tirana, Albania, and has excellent knowledge of English and moderate knowledge of German and Spanish.

Mr. Hasa can be contacted by phone at +35544500230 or by e-mail at Kris.Hasa@ffconsulting.al.
