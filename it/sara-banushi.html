Sara Banushi

Sara Banushi is a Junior Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting. Most recently she completed her second Master’s Degree in Rome, Italy, where she graduated from the University of La Sapienza with a degree in Global Regulation of Markets. Prior to her graduation from La Sapienza, Ms. Banushi had completed her Master’s in International and Commercial Law from the University of New York Tirana as Class Valedictorian as well as a degree in law from Marin Barleti University with a minor in Business Law. Currently she is continuing the legal education program at the National School of Advocacy.

During her academic studies, Ms. Banushi has completed various internships, including an internship at the People’s Advocate (Ombdusman) Institution as part of her university practice, as well as an internship at the George Marshall Albania Association. Prior to joining Frost & Fire Consulting, she completed a one-year internship at a law firm in Tirana, where she contributed mainly to civil and administrative law. As part of her hands-on experience, Ms. Banushi has contributed to the drafting of lawsuits, court documents and communications with relevant institutions.

While at Frost & Fire Consulting, Ms. Banushi’s areas of interest and involvement include commercial law, public and private international law, and arbitration. Ms. Banushi enjoys researching academic legal topics and has authored several articles in these fields, which have been published in professional magazines in Albania.

Beyond the law, Ms. Banushi likes physical activity, literature and arts. She was born in Tirana, Albania, and has excellent knowledge of English, Italian and moderate knowledge of Spanish. Ms. Banushi may be reached by phone at +355 4 450 0230 or by e-mail at
sara.banushi@ffconsulting.al.
