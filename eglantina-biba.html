Eglantina Biba

Eglantina Biba is an Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting. In 2016 Ms. Biba graduated from the Tirana Faculty of Law, where she received her Bachelors of Law and a Master of Science degree in Civil Law. In recognition of her academic achievements, she was awarded the Golden Medal, the highest academic honor in the Albanian educational system. In 2019, Ms. Biba successfully completed the initial training program at the National School of Advocacy.

At Frost & Fire Consulting Ms. Biba’s primarily focuses on the company law, corporate and securities transactions, and merger and acquisitions. Her responsibilities include the following: (i) drafting and amending various legal documents (sale and purchase agreements, employment and loan agreements, memorandums, lawsuits); (ii) drafting articles of incorporation/company statutes, share transfer agreements, increase/decrease of capital stock, shareholder agreements; (iii) representation of clients at the General Directorate of Industrial Property; (iv) representation of clients at the National Business Center for registration/establishment of companies/natural persons, registration of company acts; and (v) conducting various research projects for local or international clients.

Prior to joining Frost & Fire Consulting, Ms. Biba had continuously supplemented her academic studies with field work. She did a three-month practice as a lawyer for the Albanian Adoption Committee, had internships with local law firms, and volunteered to assist the office of the State Advocate with a project concerning the official translation of some judgements of the European Court of Human Rights. This experience further enhanced Ms. Biba’s knowledge about the Strasbourg Court case law, Comparative Law and European Union legislation. Additionally, Ms. Biba has been actively involved in several projects and activities with national and international organizations, such as a program for strengthening the justice system in Albania (JuST) organized by the United States Agency for International Development in cooperation with the Albanian National Bar Association, as well as practicing simulation models of EU Institutions, initiated by European Movement in Albania (EMA) with the assistance of European Movement International (EMI). Furthermore during the period May 2017- February 2018, Ms. Biba has been part of the Academy of European Integration and Negotiations (AEIN), a project organized with the support of the European Commission within the program Jean Monnet “Policy Debate with Academic World”. The project aimed at the preparation and information of groups that are directly affected by Albania’s process of opening accession negotiations for EU membership

Ms. Biba was born in Puka, Albania and has good knowledge of English and Italian.  She can be reached by phone at +35544500230 or by e-mail at eglantina.biba@ffconsulting.al.
