Aida Maho

Aida Maho is an Associate with Frost & Fire Consulting.  She is a recent graduate of the London School of Economics and Political Science, where she received a Masters of Laws degree in Corporate Law with very good results.  Prior to enrolling at LSE, Ms. Maho graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Law from the European University of Tirana, followed by a Master of Science Degree in Business Law from the same university, graduating at the top of her class.

Throughout the duration of her studies, Ms. Maho has worked part time to complement her theoretical knowledge with working experience.  At the European University, she was a teaching assistant for the Law Department where she conducted research in in the fields of taxation and real estate.  She also worked as a summer associate for a local law firm where she assisted foreign investors that were interested in projects involving infrastructure and the energy sector in Albania.  While there, Ms. Maho drafted contracts (including shareholder agreements or employment contracts), company statutes or articles of incorporation and various other documents.

Furthermore, Ms. Maho has been actively engaged with various projects in the civil society.  She has volunteered as EU Legal Expert at the National Forum of Young Professionals, contributing to the Albania's EU integration by organizing capacity building workshops, drafting policy papers and briefings and many other activities.  In addition, she has participated in national and international projects and conferences such as "Wheeling EU Enlargement", a project organized by the European Public Law Organization and financed by the European Commission, where students from EU and non-EU members promoted democratic values and cooperation between countries.

Ms. Maho was born in Korca, Albania and she speaks fluently English and Italian.  She can be reached by phone at +35544500230 or by email at aida.maho@ffconsulting.al.
