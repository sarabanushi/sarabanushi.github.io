Pro Bono
At Frost & Fire Consulting, we fully recognize that no matter how perfect of an environment we may try to create within our confined walls, we do not live in an island but within the society of a developing country that has many needs, not all of which can be fulfilled by the government or through paid services. That is why all of our members have a long and storied pro bono contribution to charitable, not-for-profit, and civic initiatives, including the following:

  • Ensuring that a child suffering from led poisoning would continue to get treatment at a leading hospital by helping him and his father with asylum proceedings
  • Serving as trustee and general counsel of the American University of Kosovo Foundation, an organization dedicated to supporting the not-for-profit American University in Kosovo
  • Serving as the interim president of the American University in Kosovo
  • Serving as trustee and vice chairman of the board of directors of the National Albanian American Counsel, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to advocating for Albanians' interests in Washington DC
  • Serving as trustee on the board of directors of the Albanian American Women's Organization
  • Serving on the executive committee of the Albanian Basketball Federation
  • Serving on the advisory board of the "Mjaft," an organization dedicated to increasing civic participation and awareness
  • Serving as president and as executive committee member of the Albanian Karate Federation
  • Coordinating the government's support for the environmental movement Green Line Albania
  • Representing the Civic Alliance for the Protection of the Bay of Vlora before the Aarhus Convention Compliance Committee in Geneva
  • Spearheading the creation of a master's degree in public administration in the not-for-profit "Zonja e Keshillit te Mire" University
  • Leading a project that offered sociopolitical training courses to Albanian students and postgraduated civil servants.

Frost & Fire Consulting not only endorses the pro bono engagement of our employees but also expects it. If you believe we should get involved with a worthy pro bono matter, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@ffconsulting.al.
