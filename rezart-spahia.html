Rezart Spahia

Rezart Spahia is a founding member and managing partner of Frost & Fire Consulting. Primarily a lawyer by training and experience, he has advised international and local clients on a broad range of corporate transactions for close to twenty years.

Prior to founding Frost & Fire Consulting, Mr. Spahia spent almost a decade as a corporate finance lawyer with the premiere American law firm Cravath, Swaine & Moore LLP, both in its New York and London offices. While at Cravath, Mr. Spahia worked with clients across industries on a multitude of matters including in: (i) the mergers and acquisition practice group, assisting sellers and buyers with acquisitions, dispositions or investments; (ii) the underwriting practice group, assisting underwriters and issuers with IPOs and other securities offerings; (iii) the private equity practice group, assisting investors with strategic acquisitions and investments including real estate; (iv) the banking practice group, assisting banking clients with setting up credit facilities; (v) the London office group, working with international clients on various cross-border transactions; and (vi) the general corporate practice group, advising corporate clients’ boards on a broad array of corporate governance best practices. Representative clients included IBM, Citigroup, JPMorgan Chase, Time Warner Inc., AOL, Goldman Sachs, WebMD Health Corporation, Manpower Inc., Credit Suisse, Aramark Corporation, Readers Digest, Banco Santander, Grupo Modelo, Ripplewood Holdings, Lindsay Goldberg, On Semiconductor Corporation, Enel Group, etc.

Upon his return to Albania and the founding of Frost & Fire Consulting, apart from his continued involvement with U.S. and international clients interested in the region, Mr. Spahia assists many of the most important Albanian businesses with their corporate and project financing matters, investors with joint ventures and investments across various industries, land owners and developers with real estate development transactions, clients involved in the tourism and energy sector, as well as promising start ups with their structuring and other important matters. Soon after entering legal guide directories covering Albania, Mr. Spahia has been ranked as a Leading Individual from Legal 500 and a Highly Regarded Lawyer for Corporate, M&A and Project Finance from IFLR 1000. Furthermore, Mr. Spahia contributes pro bono on the board of various not for profits, including being a trustee of the board of Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT/AUK) in Kosova, a trustee of the Ivanaj Foundations, vice-president of the Albanian Karate Federation and member of Albania’s High Council of Sports.

Mr. Spahia was born in Tirana, Albania. He received a BA in history, graduating as class valedictorian and was awarded the Gold Medal from the Albanian Ministry of Education, the highest academic honor of the time. In 2000, Mr. Spahia graduated with a Juris Doctor degree from the Northwestern School of Law of Lewis & Clark College, where he accomplished various academic achievements, including being selected as member of the Cornelious Honor Society and Phi Delta Phi International Legal Honor Society on the basis of superior scholarship and leadership. Mr. Spahia was also the only student in the school’s history to graduate with two specialty certificates in Business Law and Environmental Law. Pursuant to his JD studies, Mr. Spahia received a master of laws (LL.M.) degree in International Legal Studies with concentration in international economic law from New York University School of Law, graduating with a magna cum laude GPA equivalent, where he was also a graduate editor of the NYU Journal of International Law and Politics. Prior to embarking on his legal path, Mr. Spahia was an underwater archaeologist at the Academy of Sciences in Albania and the Institute of Nautical Archaeology at Texas A&M University.

Mr. Spahia is admitted to practice in New York and is member of the New York City Bar Association. Mr. Spahia may be reached by phone at +355 4 450 0230 or by e-mail at Rezart.Spahia@ffconsulting.al.
