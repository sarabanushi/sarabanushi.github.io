Alfonc Rakaj

Alfonc Rakaj is an Analyst with Frost & Fire Consulting. He brings on board a versatile educational and working experience in the private, public and non-profit organizations.

Mr. Rakaj was born in Malësia e Madhe, Albania but has lived most of his adult life in the United States and the United Kingdom where he completed his secondary and post-secondary education. He received a Master of Arts in International Relations from King's College London, a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and a Bachelor of Arts in International Studies from Oregon State University in the United States.

During and in between his studies in the United States Mr. Rakaj worked as a Congressional Intern for Congressman Eliot Engel in the U.S. Congress. He also interned at the Scottish Parliament in addition to serving as a Campus Coordinator for the U.S. State Department. After graduating college, Mr. Rakaj worked as a Political Risk Consultant in New York for a start-up, using his political science background to provide meticulously researched country reports for western investors seeking opportunities in emerging economies. Upon his return to Albania, Mr. Rakaj continued to be involved in the public sector by interning in the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

In addition to his experience in the public sector in three different countries, Mr. Rakaj has contributed to domestic and foreign non-governmental organizations, including Amnesty International in London. During his involvements with NGOs both in Albania and abroad, Mr. Rakaj was able to apply his regional knowledge of local dynamics to promote the advancement of human rights and democratic reforms in Albania and the Western Balkans.

Mr. Rakaj made his transition to the private sector by working as an Operations Analyst at Platinium Investment, a non-banking financial institution providing alternative investment products for the Albanian market. While there, he managed a team of five and was responsible for overseeing the business operations of the company to ensure compliance with industry regulations. Most recently Mr. Rakaj worked as a freelance Consultant for a leading global consulting firm specializing in sustainable change. In this role, Mr. Rakaj honed his skills in implementation consulting, analyzing, contributing and supporting the implementation of sustainable changes for companies seeking to boost productivity, efficiency and reduce waste.
